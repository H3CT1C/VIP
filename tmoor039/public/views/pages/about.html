What is VIP?

VIP (Vertically Integrated Projects) is a cumulative effort between staff, graduate students, and undergraduate students to participate in long term research projects. It seeks to: create long-term research & development experiences, cultivates leadership & mentoring and benefits faculty research programs. articipation in the program is not limited to students in engineering but all disciplines are encouraged to participate. The goal of the project is to have any level undergraduate students and faculty work together solving real world problems in a multidisciplinary environment. Students earn academic credits and technical experience.
